Located in Dubai, 6.2 mi from Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo, Vida Creek Harbor provides accommodations with two restaurants, free private parking and a fitness center. All rooms feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom. The accommodations features a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

All units in the hotel are equipped with a kettle.

A continental breakfast is available daily at Vida Creek Harbor.

The accommodations offers 4-star accommodations.

Dubai Mall is 6.2 mi from Vida Creek Harbor, while The Dubai Fountain is 6.8 mi away. The nearest airport is Dubai International Airport, 11 mi from the hotel.

Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.1 for a two-person trip.
